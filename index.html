About Me

My name is Gavin Howard from Austin, TX, and I am writing to you because of my interest in playing basketball at the collegiate level. I play high school basketball in Cedar Park, TX for Vista Ridge High School and play in the summer for Houston Hoops CTX 17U Miller. I am 6'4", 186 pounds, and working to add muscle weight this summer.

About myself, I am a wing, play shooting guard, small forward and power forward positions on my school and club teams - I guard 1-5 on the court and help bring the ball up to run the offense. In my Junior year, I received 1st team All-District in Region 25-6A. For my upcoming senior year, my goal is to earn District MVP honors. I was on and started varsity in my sophomore and junior years in high school and was named a team captain in both years. On my school team, my goal is to lead by example, include the younger players and build the team as a whole in an effort to gain respect of my teammates and create a positive team environment. Going into my Senior year, the goals I have for my high school career are to obtain 1,250 points and help lead our program into playoffs for the first time since 2017.

As a student, I am a member of the National Honor Society and currently have a 3.9 GPA and 4.9 Weighted GPA. I scored a 1260 on my SAT - 600 writing, 660 math. I also earned and will continue to earn dual credit hours taking classes through my local community college.

I appreciate your consideration and, if interested, I would enjoy the opportunity to meet and discuss the possibility of joining your team after I graduate in 2026. Please let me know if there is anything further you would like to see or know about me to determine if I would be a good fit for your program. Given the opportunity, I am confident I would have a positive impact on your program.
